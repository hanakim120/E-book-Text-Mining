Effective software project management is the fundamental remedy for the schedule delay, cost overrun, and customer complaints.  There are two phases of activities for software project management: planning activities and tracking and controlling activities. As the planning is conducted during the early stage of requirement analysis, there are many difficulties for estimating size, cost and schedule. Due to unrealistic software development plans and WBS, project managers neglect to collect objective progress data. In this study, effective methods for software project management, which can be adopted by software development organizations, are presented.
