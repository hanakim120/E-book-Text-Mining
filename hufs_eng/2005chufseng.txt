Since the website of a university is a portal of various users such as students, professors, faculty, prospective students, alumni, and other general users, it can be different from the website of a company, and should be developed more carefully. In developing the website of a university, the capability of providing communication channel between the users is essential as well as providing necessary information to the users. Whether the website can convey or not the image of the university enough well to the prospective students is also important function of the website.
In this paper, we review the development process of a university website. Through the case of Hankuk Universtiy of Foreign Studies, which opened a new website in commemoration of its 50th anniversary, the whole process including the background, preliminary stages, and follow-up is shortly discussed.
