Total Quality Management (TQM) has been widely accepted as a strategic method to improve competitiveness of an organization. To satisfy customer requirements, all employees should participate on continuous improvement activities of their own processes. TQM provides a new paradigm encompassing interests of society, customer and all member of an organization. TQM includes various specific techniques to solve real problems and contains unique philosophy and principles which are distinctive from other management practices. Leadership, information and analysis, human resource management and process management has been considered as common denominators of essential constructs of TQM and customer focus and strategic planning becomes important constructs of TQM. Even though there have been many controversies, the effectiveness of TQM programs is validated through various case studies and empirical researches.
