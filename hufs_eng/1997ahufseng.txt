Due to the rapid improvements of telecommunications technology and increased pressure on the opening up the markets, the environments of telecommunications market have been changed from a monopolistic one which is governed by providers into a competitive one which is governed by customers.  The major focus of telecommunications quality management was conformance to government regulations, but it has been shifted to effective analysis and fulfillment of customer requirements.  Therefore, even though there are some difficulties for the implementation of service quality management, many advanced telecommunications operators adopted it as an essential strategy for the improvement of their competitiveness.  For the development of an effective quality management system of telecommunications operators, problems and differences of service quality management will be discussed , and the basic concept of QOS/NP (Quality of Service/Network Performance) and an effective data collection framework which is necessary for the quality management will be discussed, and a QOS definition framework and a QOS cycle which can analyze and categorize customer requirements will be presented.
